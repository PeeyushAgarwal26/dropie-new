Services

Education

The "Dropie" is an innovative learning solution for students to enhance their programming skills. It consists of a physical robot that teaches programming concepts in a fun and interactive way, and a software system that keeps records of students' progress and performance. The system also includes a student-helping app with an AI chatbot that can solve their doubts instantly.

Medical

The Dropie robot performs various nursing tasks, such as taking vital signs, administering medication, and assisting with daily activities. The software system keeps records of patients' health and medical history, allowing for more personalized care. In addition, it includes an app with an AI chatbot that can provide solutions to patients' health concerns based on their symptoms.
